Who We Are

Built by students,
powered by the cloud.

AWS Cloud Club - Flurry is an official AWS Cloud Club chapter dedicated to equipping students with real-world cloud skills through workshops, community, and certification support.

Our Mission

To empower students with hands-on AWS cloud skills, foster a collaborative learning community, and guide members toward earning industry-recognized certifications that open real career opportunities.

Our Vision

To be the leading student cloud community in the region — where every member leaves with the skills, certifications, and network to thrive in the cloud industry.

Our Values

Community over competition. Hands-on over passive learning. Openness to beginners. Collaboration across chapters. And a genuine drive to help every member grow.

AWS Official Chapter

Recognized and supported by AWS, we are part of a national network of AWS Cloud Clubs — connected to resources, mentors, and opportunities that individual students rarely get access to.

Our Story

How Flurry started

Flurry began as a small group of IT and CS students who wanted more than what the classroom offered. Frustrated by the gap between academic theory and real-world cloud skills, a handful of students decided to build a community around learning AWS — properly, hands-on.

What started as informal study sessions quickly grew into a full AWS Cloud Club chapter, officially recognized by Amazon Web Services. Today, Flurry runs structured workshops, exam prep bootcamps, and inter-chapter collaborations — and we're just getting started.

Organization Structure

Club Hierarchy & Roles

Every role in Flurry is designed to provide hands-on leadership experience while serving the community.

Captain

Secretary

Manages documentation, schedules, and club communications.

Assistant Secretary

Supports the Secretary in administrative tasks and records.

Marketing & Creatives

Relations
